Arrange the way you think

{{APP_NAME}} is a spatial, infinite-canvas desktop app for people running multi-stage projects across different media. Place notes, documents, images, audio, video, drawings, and tasks anywhere on a canvas, link them, and group them — arranged the way you actually think, not the way an app's menus make you.

And it's not a metaphor: the files on your canvas are your real files, and the groups you make are real folders on your disk. Organize spatially and you organize your actual filesystem.

📁
The Canvas IS Your Filesystem
The notes, documents, and assets on your canvas are real files on your drive. Move a module on the canvas and its file moves. Draw a group and a real folder is created on your disk. Local-first, offline, no lock-in.
✦
Spatial Freedom & Visual Links
Place anything anywhere. Organize by proximity, size, and visual connection rather than nested folders or lists. Draw connection lines between modules to trace multi-directional project flows.
⚛
Everything in One Place
Keep Markdown notes, PDF and EPUB documents, images, audio waveforms, video playbacks, task boards, and vector drawings coexisting on the same board. No context-switching between different apps.
⚡
Restrained & Responsive
A minimal, distraction-free environment built to stay smooth and performant as your workspace grows. Every file is stored in a clean, open format that external tools can read and write.

Why {{APP_NAME}} is different

The canvas is your real files

No importing or exporting to a proprietary database. What you see is a live reflection of your local files and folders. Organise the canvas, organize your hard drive.

Versatile spatial primitives

Versatility through simple, combinable primitives (modules, links, groups, canvases) rather than a rigid, bloated pile of features.

Local-first, you own it

No user accounts, no subscriptions, no cloud syncing, no telemetry. Your files remain on your disk, always accessible in open standard formats.

Yours to shape

Whether you like a clean, sparse canvas or a busy, information-dense hub. The app provides visual layout control without imposing a style.
